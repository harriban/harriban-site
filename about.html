About Us

Sh. Surinder Gupta

Harriban Broadcasting and Communications was founded in the late 90s by our Founder and Director Mr Surinder Gupta, an alumnus of BITS, Pilani. Since our incorporation, Mr. Gupta and his team have been relentlessly working to fulfill his vision to serve his country by bringing the best technologies and products from around the work to meet the growing demands of the broadcasting and communication industry.

His vision and leadership qualities were instrumental in extending India's radio and television reach across the length and breadth of the country including far-flung places of the country.

Despite being a highly successful entrepreneur, Mr. Gupta is a very humble, down-to-earth person who is deeply connected to his roots with high regard for ethics and values both at work and in his personal life.
